By: Kale Miller On: Thu 04 May 2023
In: Random
Tags: 
print("Hello, world!")

It has been a recent goal of mine to start to unload a few more of my thoughts. It's very easy to get caught up in the day-to-day of life and never stop to write down all of the wisdom you accumulate over time.

I hope that this blog will become a sink for everything going on in my mind: coding tricks and techniques I learn, random thoughts from my walks, and experiences that I think would be selfish for me to keep to myself.

I hope that not only do I look at this blog and become proud of the work I put in, but you too are able to take something away from it of value.
